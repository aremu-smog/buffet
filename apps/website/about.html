Serve yourself

Sometime last year, I started thinking of a way to send money to people without asking for their account number because I am a ‘shy’ person and I didn’t want to come off as assuming people need(ed) the measly 5,000NGN I was planning to give out so I thought. What if there’s a place where people could go put their account details and get money without my involvement?

That’s what sparked the idea but unfortunately, I didn’t have the time to build the project until I started learning Ruby on Rails this year and I thought this would be a great project to horn my Ruby skills.

At the moment, I am the sole donor to this project, if you would like to contribute/donate to this project. Please checkout the Contribute page
